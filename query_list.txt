# Query List for Testing the NLP Query Engine

# --- Basic Queries ---
# Simple COUNT and SELECT
"How many employees are there?"
"List all employees."
"Show me the staff table."
"Who are the employees?"

# --- Filter Queries ---
# Simple WHERE clause
"Show me all developers in the Engineering department."
"List employees hired this year."
"Which employees are based in the New York office?"
"Who reports to Jane Doe?"

# --- Aggregation Queries ---
# AVG, GROUP BY
"What is the average salary by department?"
"Show the average compensation for each role."
"Count of employees per division."

# --- Complex Queries ---
# TOP N, ORDER BY
"Who are the top 5 highest paid employees?"
"Find the top 3 longest-serving employees."

# Hybrid (SQL + Document) Queries
"Show me performance reviews for engineers hired last year."
"Find resumes of employees with Python skills earning over 100k."
"Which departments have the highest turnover according to exit interview documents?"

# --- Document-Only Queries ---
"What is the company policy on remote work?"
"Find John Doe's resume."
"Summarize the employee handbook."

# --- Edge Cases ---
# Ambiguous Query
"Show me John."

# Query with potential for no results
"What is the salary for contractors?"

# Complex Aggregation
"What are the month-over-month hiring trends?"
