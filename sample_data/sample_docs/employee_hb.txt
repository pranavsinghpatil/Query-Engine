Employee Handbook

Vacation Policy
Full-time employees are entitled to 20 days of paid vacation per calendar year. All vacation requests must be submitted to and approved by a manager at least two weeks in advance.

Sick Leave Policy
Employees are granted 10 days of paid sick leave annually. If an employee is sick for more than three consecutive days, a doctor's note is required to validate the absence.

Remote Work Policy
Remote work is available upon manager approval. Employees working remotely are expected to be available and responsive during normal business hours.
