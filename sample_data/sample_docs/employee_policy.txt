Employee Handbook - Company Policies

Welcome to our company! This handbook outlines important policies and procedures.

**Section 1: General Conduct**
Employees are expected to maintain a professional demeanor at all times. Harassment of any kind will not be tolerated.

**Section 2: Compensation and Benefits**
Salaries are reviewed annually. Employees are eligible for health insurance, paid time off (PTO), and a 401k retirement plan. Details on compensation structure and benefits enrollment can be found on the HR portal.

**Section 3: Performance Reviews**
Performance reviews are conducted semi-annually. Managers will provide feedback and set goals for employee development.

**Section 4: Remote Work Policy**
Our company supports flexible work arrangements, including remote work. Employees must ensure a secure and productive home office environment.
